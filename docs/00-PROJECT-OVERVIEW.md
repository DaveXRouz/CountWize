# CountWize Project Overview

## What Is This Project?

CountWize is a **cryptocurrency recovery services company website**. It is the main marketing/informational website (NOT the dashboard/app). The website helps people who have lost access to their cryptocurrency through scams, forgotten passwords, wrong transactions, or hacked wallets to recover their assets.

1. **Crypto Recovery Service** - Recover lost or stolen cryptocurrency
2. **Crypto Scam Recovery** - Trace fraudulent transactions, identify scammers
3. **Wallet Recovery** - Recover access to locked wallets
4. **Blockchain Analytics** - Transaction tracing and analysis
5. **Legal Support** - Guidance on reporting to authorities
6. **Exchange Communication** - Liaison with exchanges
7. **Crypto Tax Consulting** - Tax calculation and optimization ($500-$1,500 packages)
