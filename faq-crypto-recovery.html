About Count Wise

What services does Count Wise offer?
We provide tools for crypto tax calculations, compliance, and secure data management tailored to your jurisdiction.
What types of crypto transactions are supported?
We support trades, staking rewards, airdrops, and other common crypto transactions.
Is Count Wise compatible with multiple wallets and exchanges?
Yes, Count Wise supports integration with multiple wallets and crypto exchanges, allowing you to consolidate your transaction history in one place for seamless tax calculations.
How does Count Wise calculate crypto taxes?
We use real-time market pricing and apply industry-standard cost-basis methods (FIFO/LIFO) plus jurisdiction-specific rules to compute your gains and losses accurately.
Can Count Wise handle complex crypto portfolios?
Yes, our tools are designed to manage complex portfolios with multiple wallets and exchanges.
How does Count Wise handle wallet integrations?
Our platform seamlessly integrates with popular wallets like MetaMask and Ledger to retrieve transaction data securely.

We use advanced encryption and follow industry-leading practices to protect your information.

No, our pricing is transparent, and all costs are clearly outlined before you start.
Can Count Wise generate tax reports for different jurisdictions?
Yes, we automatically format compliant reports for the US, UK, EU, Canada and many other jurisdictions.
