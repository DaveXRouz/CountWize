This means that they cannot be canceled if you specify the wrong network or address after confirming the transaction in the blockchain.

This feature often leads to the loss of valuable assets without the possibility of their recovery.

First steps after losing
access to your crypto

If crypto wallet access lost – don’t be upset, because you can still change the situation and return your savings. However, to do this, you need to make the right decisions quickly. What exactly should be done, you will learn below.

Don’t panic

Who is your worst enemy? Panic. Panic and all the other emotions it generates. Whatever happens, you cannot change it if you are guided by feelings and not by sound reason.

If a mistake is made, stop and assess the situation. A correct assessment is the first step to preventing a catastrophe. Anger and rage are not the best allies in any situation. And when your money is at stake, you definitely do not want to be guided by these feelings.
‍
Pull yourself together and do not waste much time on this. At this stage, you can assess the likelihood of independently restore Ethereum, Bitcoin, Litecoin, and other cryptocurrencies.

01

Stop all related activity

Once you have gotten rid of your emotions and overcome panic, stop all actions. Just pause and breathe. Now, you need to act very clearly and start correcting the situation.
‍
Notify the people who planned to transfer cryptocurrency about the incident, stop all ongoing transactions (if possible), and break any ties with the scammers (do not communicate with them, do not try to contact them, do not agree to the conditions put forward, etc.).
‍
Such a set of actions will help minimize losses and avoid additional troubles. In addition, do not log in to suspicious platforms that are supposedly able to help instantly restore access to the wallet or return the stolen funds to you.

02

Gather all available information

The next step is collecting evidence and recording traces of fraudulent activity. To do this, you must save everything relevant to the case in a safe place. Wallet addresses, transaction IDs, exchange names, screenshots, and email addresses are a small part of everything requiring special attention.
‍
All this information may be useless, but sometimes it is the key to success. Sometimes, it will help you quickly identify the fraudsters and return the stolen funds or access to the wallet. Recording most of the data should be done as soon as possible, since the attackers can delete or hide it in a few minutes.
‍
If you cannot obtain valuable information, then at least collect your wallet data. They can also help in returning the cryptocurrency.

03

Check if you still have partial access

This is the moment of truth; perhaps luck is on your side. Sometimes, a lack of access to the wallet does not mean its loss. This opens up additional opportunities for the affected cryptocurrency owners and makes it possible to minimize financial losses.

To try to restore access, you can use a recovery phrase, special backup passwords, email verification, and other suggested options. Of course, not all of them will be available, but at least one will work.
‍
In some cases, you may still have full access to the wallet and its assets (for example, if the scammers withdraw part of the funds after the hack). If this happens, you must immediately take advantage of the situation and transfer all savings to a safe address. This way, you will save some of the funds and minimize the negative consequences of the hack or your carelessness.

04

Run a quick risk assessment

Understanding the problem is half the success of solving it. This rule applies in all cases, including when access to cryptocurrency is lost.
‍
In this situation, understanding the reasons for the problem helps choose the correct action algorithm and increases the chances of returning valuable assets. Having identified a hack, found evidence of an incorrect transfer, or realized that you could not get to the cryptocurrency due to a forgotten password, you will know precisely how to proceed and will not make many other mistakes.

Understanding the reason for losing access to your wallet or savings will also simplify and speed up the recovery procedure in cases where you seek help from specialists.

05

Contact professionals as early as possible

As practice shows, the effective window for correcting the situation when losing your cryptocurrency or access to your wallet is 48 hours. During this time, especially with prompt contact with professionals, the chances of correcting the situation remain the highest.
‍
After that, the probability of a positive outcome decreases exponentially, and it will simply disappear in a couple of days. In truth, remember that every hour counts, and plan your actions based on this truth.

06

It is challenging to restore access to cryptocurrency on your own. Even if you are an expert in this matter and know many secrets, you will still be unable to cope with the task without a team of professionals.
