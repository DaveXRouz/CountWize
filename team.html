Common Questions About CountWize

How does CountWize work?

CountWize connects securely to your crypto wallets to aggregate transactions and calculate tax obligations based on your jurisdiction.

Is CountWize safe to use?

Yes, CountWize uses encrypted connections and does not store private keys or access your funds.

What types of crypto transactions can I report?

You can calculate taxes for trades, staking rewards, airdrops, and more.

Are there any fees for using CountWize?

We offer flexible pricing based on the features you need. No hidden fees.

How do you handle my data?

Your data is used only for tax calculations and is stored securely. We follow strict privacy standards.

Do I still need an accountant?

CountWize simplifies tax reporting, but you may consult a tax professional for specific legal advice.
